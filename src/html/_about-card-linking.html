About Card Linking

Swellcoin uses the same card linking methods as Mint, Quickbooks and PayPal via our partners Finicity and Experian. As leading financial security experts and trusted partners with banks nationwide, Finicity / Experian do all the heavy lifting to link your cards and secure a copy of your digital transactions (purchases and returns). They are then provided to Swellcoin in a read-only format to calculate your cashback, all with bank level security. Swellcoin never has access to your account(s) or credentials in any manner whatsoever. You may un-link your card(s) at any time, or add or change cards as desired.

For more detailed information, please see our Security Statement.
